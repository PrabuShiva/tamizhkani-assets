STEP 4

Connect Domain to Netlify

In Netlify:

1

Go to Domain Management

Site Settings → Domain Management → Add Custom Domain

2

Click "Pending DNS verification"

Netlify shows you the IP address: 75.2.60.5

In GoDaddy/Namecheap:

3

Go to DNS Records

Find the DNS Management page for your domain

4

Add these 2 records:

Type: A | Name: @ | Value: 75.2.60.5
Type: CNAME | Name: www | Value: your-site.netlify.app
5

Wait 15 mins – 24 hours

Your domain will point to your Netlify website! 🎉

⚠️ Delete any "Parked" A records in GoDaddy before adding Netlify's A record!

STEP 5

Enable Free SSL Certificate 🔒

SSL makes your website secure (https://) and shows the 🔒 padlock in the browser. Netlify gives it for FREE!

1

Go to Domain Management in Netlify

Scroll down to HTTPS section

2

Click "Verify DNS Configuration"

Netlify checks if your domain is pointing correctly

3

Click "Provision Certificate"

Netlify installs a free Let's Encrypt SSL certificate

4

Wait 5–15 minutes

Your site is now secure with https:// ✅

🔒

BONUS

Add a Working Contact Form

Use Netlify Forms — no backend needed, completely free!

<form name="contact" netlify>
  <input type="hidden" name="form-name" value="contact"/>
  <input type="text" name="name" placeholder="Your Name"/>
  <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Your Email"/>
  <textarea name="message"></textarea>
  <button type="submit">Send</button>
</form>

⚠️ The magic is the netlify attribute in the form tag! That's all you need.

1

Add netlify attribute to form

Just add the word "netlify" inside your <form> tag

2

Deploy to Netlify

Netlify auto-detects the form when you upload your site

3

Enable Form Detection

Go to Netlify → Forms → Enable form detection

4

View submissions

All form submissions are stored in Netlify → Forms dashboard
